The Four Roles

Retailer

Sees customer orders and its own inventory. Decides: order to Wholesaler. Ships: to customers (backlog if short).

Wholesaler

Sees Retailer's orders and its own state. Decides: order to Distributor. Ships: to Retailer.

Distributor

Sees Wholesaler's orders and its own state. Decides: order to Factory. Ships: to Wholesaler.

Factory

Releases production (with a lead time) before it can ship. Decides: weekly production release. Ships: to Distributor.

The Bullwhip Effect

Small changes in customer demand create wild swings as they move up the supply chain. Each player, acting rationally on their own, creates chaos for the system.
